Welcome to NU Event Hub

Our Mission

At NU Event Hub, our mission is simple: to foster a vibrant, engaging community at Northeastern University. We aim to make it easier for every student to discover, manage, and enjoy campus events, thus enriching their university life.

What We Offer

  • Event Discovery: Dive into a wide array of events with ease. Whether you're looking for academic talks, social gatherings, or career fairs, our intuitive platform makes finding your next campus event a breeze.
  • Event Management: Got an event to share? Log in and take charge. Our platform allows you to create, update, and manage your events effortlessly, ensuring you reach your audience effectively.
  • Ticket Management: From sales to refunds, handle all aspects of your event tickets securely and efficiently. Our robust system supports all your ticketing needs, making event access smoother for everyone.

Join Us

Get involved and stay connected with all that Northeastern University has to offer. Explore, create, and participate with NU Event Hub — where your campus events come to life!
