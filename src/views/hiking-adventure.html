Description:

Experience this 2.2-mile loop trail near Hayward, California. Generally considered an easy route, it takes an average of 55 min to complete. This is a popular trail for hiking, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day. The trail is open year-round and is beautiful to visit anytime. Dogs are welcome and may be off-leash in some areas.
