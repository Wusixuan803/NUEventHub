Description:

Join the exhilarating NU Code Hackathon at Northeastern University's Tech Lab on February 15, 2025. This 30-hour coding marathon, hosted by Khoury College of Computer Sciences, invites participants to develop innovative software solutions to real-world challenges. Gain invaluable experience through workshops, mentorship, and collaboration with tech enthusiasts. Compete for prizes in creativity and technological innovation. Whether you're a novice or an expert coder, this event promises intense coding, learning, and networking opportunities. Come, code, and possibly win!
