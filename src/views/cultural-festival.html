Northeastern Cultural Festival 2025

Created by:

Northeastern University Student Union

Location:

Tea Shop, Northeastern University Oakland Campus, 5000 MacArthur Blvd, Oakland, CA 94613

Date:

Sunday, February 23, 2025

Time:

11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Description:

This lively event, hosted by the Northeastern University Student Union, showcases cultural performances, global cuisine, and art from around the world. Enjoy a day filled with music, dance, food, and interactive workshops suitable for all ages. Explore and celebrate the rich diversity of cultures at Northeastern University in this educational and fun-filled festival!
